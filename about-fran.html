A love letter to flowers, people, and place

Francine Miller’s love for flowers began in San Francisco, where she grew up inspired by neighborhood markets and small, everyday moments of beauty. To her, flowers were never just decorations — they were a way to express care, celebration, and connection.

After training in floral design and working with local florists, Francine opened Fran’s Flowers in 1995. From the beginning, her goal was simple: create a welcoming space where everyone feels at home.

Nearly thirty years later, Francine still hand-crafts each arrangement with intention, favoring seasonal blooms and local growers. She believes in slowing down, designing thoughtfully, and giving back — which is why a portion of every sale supports community efforts across the Bay Area.

“If a bouquet can make someone feel seen, then I’ve done my job.”
~ Francine Miller
